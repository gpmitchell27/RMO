This project consisted of using Audition CS6 and GarageBand to make a radio spot for a ficticous radio station. To do this I had to make my own voice recordings and then manipulate them with effects to make them pop.

Three different views are used to manipulate data in Audition.Spectral, Wave, and Multi-track. Multi-Track allows you to deal with your project almost like iMovie and Premiere. You can view all the files at once -- with wave you can edit individual clips by themselves adn with spectral you can heal your clips by taking out unwanted noise.
