Radio Broadcast

This project was created and edited in Audition CS6. I have never used Audition before and I must say it was fun to manipulate audio. The project was for a ficticious radio station introduing tracks and recording lead in and fills. I used various techniques like fade in and out, use rubberbands to effect sounds in different ways and different filters to change pitch as well as normalize audio.
