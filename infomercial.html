BACK

Author:Greg Mitchell

Duration:1 min 31 seconds

This project consisted of using Adobie Premiere and GarageBand. First I had to import the files into Premiere then I had to trim and adjust each one to fit. I had to use some effects from the effects panels and also add transitions to make a more finished look.

I also had to adjust the audio for this project using fade in and out. A nice feature of Premiere was the use of markers to mark in time where the different transitions needed to take place.
